MateLink Influencer Agreement

Welcome to the MateLink Influencer Program. This agreement will determine the rights and obligations between MateLink influencers ("you") and MateLink.Please read the entire agreement carefully before signing. This agreement will take effect once you sign it by checking the box, clicking the confirmation button, or other acknowledgment. Once you become a MateLink influencer by meeting the terms and conditions of this agreement, you can receive licensing fees as agreed and create your personal account on MateLink to use relevant features and services.
I. Eligibility Requirements
1. You must be of legal adult age and have full civil capacity as defined by the laws of your country or region.
2. You must have a recognizable level of influence on social media platforms like traditional media, new media, etc. as acknowledged by MateLink.
II. Joining Process
1. In order to access platform services, you need to download the "MateLink" APP and register a "MateLink" account with your real personal information.
2、2. You can click [Me] > [App for MateLink Influencer] (subject to the page display) and submit relevant information according to the page requirements. If not approved, you can reapply after 7 days. MateLink has the right to reject or cancel registrations that provide false information or violate laws and regulations. The consequences and losses caused by users providing false information or illegal registration shall be borne by the users themselves.
III. Authorization
1. Content of Authorization
1.1 Within 3 days of this agreement taking effect, you shall provide MateLink via email with: at least 10 minutes of audio recording of your voice; at least 100 photos of your portrait; and at least 20 videos containing your voice and image.
There are no restrictions on the content of the recording. Please record in a quiet indoor environment. The voice should be smooth and clear, with a volume maintained between 50-60 decibels, and the format should be MP3.
The photos can include various types such as personal life, art, etc. They should be clear front-facing portraits of yourself from different angles, not smaller than 1080*1080 in size, and with a resolution not lower than 150. Photos with copyright risks should not be provided.
The videos should mainly showcase your daily life, the format should be MP4, and the resolution should not be lower than 1920*1080. Videos with copyright risks should not be provided.
1.2 To ensure continuous AI training, you shall provide MateLink with new recordings, photos, and videos meeting the above criteria annually during the term of our cooperation.You irrevocably authorize MateLink to use the above recordings, images, videos, and other personal information and data collected from public channels to train AI models and generate corresponding results.
1.3 MateLink has the right to apply the AI training results for commercial activities and obtain corresponding benefits.Your authorization includes but is not limited to the right of reproduction, publication, modification, distribution, lease, exhibition, performance, screening, broadcasting, dissemination via information networks, production, adaptation, translation, compilation.
2. Nature, Duration and Scope of Authorization
2.1 Your authorization is exclusive to MateLink.
2.2 The duration of your authorization is within five years from the effective date of this agreement. After the expiration of the authorization period, this does not affect MateLink's continued use of the AI training results produced during the authorization period, nor does it affect MateLink's users' purchases and usage of functions and products developed through the aforementioned AI training results.
2.3 Unless agreed otherwise, the term of authorization will automatically renew for additional 10-year periods upon expiration of the initial term or any renewal term.
3. Authorization Fee
3.1 MateLink will determine different proportionate authorization fees based on the user's influence on media, self-media, and other social platforms. You can view this from [Influencer Hub] – [Earnings] (subject to page display).
3.2 The calculation of the authorization fee is based on the costs paid by MateLink users for buying and using the related functions and products developed after AI training using the materials provided by the users.
3.3 If there is any dispute regarding the calculation of the authorization fee, you have the right to request MateLink to provide statement of account.
3.4 Authorization fees are paid through Paypal or bank card settlements. You need to fill out your information at [Influencer Certificated] - [Payment Account] (subject to page display).
3.5 You should bear the taxes that you should be responsible for in accordance with the laws applicable to you.
